Websites & E-Commerce

My process for building websites depends on your needs. I try to find balance between “doing it right” and “getting things done”.

I've been doing websites for over 10 years, and have implemented numerous custom web apps. From small brochure sites to large e-commerce implementations, I've both coded and/or managed a team to execute solutions.

Whether by architecting a plan, directing a team or coding software, I can get you to your goal. I will help you navigate through topics such as: responsive vs adaptive websites, WCAG (A, AA, AAA compliance), PCI compliance, infrastructure patterns, development best practices, database implementations (NoSQL vs RDBS) or anything else that may be important to your needs.

Mobile Apps

Everyone loves apps. They’re easy to download on your mobile phone and can do anything from enhancing your selfies to assisting in medical practices.

But not all apps are created equally. I can walk you through the various options (web vs hybrid vs native), their advantages, disadvantages and risks.

Because my strength is back-end development, I can also help and interface your app with a web application that can let people interact with information pulled from your service such as latest news, corporate reports, stock prices, etc.

Or we can help people take better selfies. It’s your call.

Digital/Touch Display

Digital display and touch screen solutions can make engaging your audience easier. Unlike a poster, they’re interactive.

I’ve built both touch screen systems and digital displays that pull in various content (social media, video, ads, etc.), controllable through a web interface.

One such system was created for The River Calgary. A large touch screen solution that allowed visitors to walk through and explore the future state of this beautiful project.

If you need menu boards, social media screens, beer boards, or a combination of all of these, let’s get creative!

Custom Code

If you’re a startup or already know what you’re building but you need an extra set of hands, I can help through contracting services.

I’m a language agnostic developer. I use the tool that’s right for the job. I’m comfortable with PHP, HTML5, SASS, JavaScript, Objective-C, C++, Swift, and various web/mobile technologies within those domains (jQuery, AngularJS, NodeJS, ExpressJS, Laravel, Drupal, etc).

While I’m happy with front-end development, my value comes from back-end integration and server ops. My workflow includes using Vagrant to match my local dev. environment to your production environment. I use GIT and am comfortable with the entire LAMP stack on Ubuntu/CentOS systems. I’ve worked with numerous databases, including NoSQL db’s such as Mongo and Couchbase. I’m happy working with REST/Payment API’s.

If you see anything that might be a fit, or have more questions, drop me a line.
